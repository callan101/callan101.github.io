About Callan 101 Sheldon

Background

Greetings! I'm Callan Sheldon, a native of the picturesque town of Petaluma, California. My journey led me to Eugene, Oregon, after graduating high school in February 2022, where I embarked on an exciting new chapter.

Passions and Pursuits

My passion lies in the digital realm, particularly in the immersive world of virtual reality games like Echo VR. I find immense joy in exploring virtual landscapes and pushing the boundaries of imagination. Beyond the digital frontier, I indulge in the tangible world by building computers and delving into the intricate workings of electronics, one component at a time.

In addition to my tech pursuits, I'm an enthusiastic graphic designer and photographer. Through my lens, I capture life's moments and transform ordinary scenes into extraordinary visual stories, exploring my creativity along the way.

Academic Journey

I'm currently pursuing my studies in computer science and computer programming in central Oregon. My academic journey fuels my curiosity and provides a strong foundation for my technological endeavors.

Interests

Apart from my love for technology, I enjoy engaging in various activities such as badminton and rock climbing. I'm also passionate about music, reading, and experimenting with amateur cinema drones and FPV drones, adding a touch of adventure to my life.

Join Me

Join me on my quest for knowledge, creativity, and innovation. Together, let's explore the endless possibilities that the digital world, arts, and technology have to offer.
